About me

Hi there! My name is Anaïs. I'm a french teacher an Unusual french teacher.

Creating is a real imperative for me. My texts, podcasts, exercises are original lessons, inspired by the french ways of speaking and behaving.

By combining my two great passions – writing fiction for both adults and children, and teaching French to non – natives, the concept of onefrenchjourney.com was born!
